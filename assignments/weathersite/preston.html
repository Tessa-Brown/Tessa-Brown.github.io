The Best Way to Welcome Spring

By Springy Springer

There are a variety of ways to welcome the warm weather to our small town of Preston, Idaho. With Spring on the horizon, it's never too early to start thinking about how you want to spend your your time outside! I've compiled a list of some of my favorite things that Idaho has to offer in the warmer months.

  • Find a friend and go for a hike.
  • Head over to the lake and try stand up paddleboarding.
  • Take a trip to the Snake River for some exhilirating rafting!
  • Find a local ranch that offers horseback-riding classes.
  • Plant a garden.
  • Take an outdoor fitness class.
  • Go on a bike ride.
  • Find a hole-in-the-wall ice cream shop.
  • Grab your clubs and go golfing.

I hope some of these ideas get you excited about the warmer weather heading our way!
